About

Vectora Consulting is an independent advisory firm specialising in cost optimisation and commercial efficiency for organisations across the Middle East.

We partner with leadership teams to identify, quantify, and realise sustainable cost savings across operating expenses, vendor contracts, product portfolios, and go-to-market models. Our work is practical, data-led, and focused on financial impact rather than theoretical recommendations.

Engagements are selective and hands-on, supporting enterprises, telecom operators, and large organisations across the GCC and wider Middle East.

Cost Optimisation Focus Areas

  • Commercial & Vendor Cost Optimisation – Contract reviews, vendor benchmarking, renegotiation strategies, and commercial model redesign
  • Product & Portfolio Rationalisation – Identifying underperforming products, reducing complexity, and improving margin contribution
  • Go-to-Market Cost Efficiency – Pricing, packaging, channel economics, and sales cost optimisation
  • Operating Model & Cost Structure Review – Identifying structural inefficiencies across teams, processes, and delivery models
  • Business Case & Savings Realisation – Quantified savings, implementation roadmaps, and tracking mechanisms to ensure benefits are realised

How We Work

  • Data-led diagnostics to identify cost leakage and prioritise high-impact opportunities
  • Clear financial quantification — savings are modelled, not assumed
  • Hands-on execution supporting negotiations, decisions, and implementation
  • Focused engagements designed to deliver tangible results within months

Typical Outcomes

  • 5–15% reduction in addressable operating and commercial costs
  • Improved vendor terms and contract flexibility
  • Simplified product portfolios with clearer margin accountability
  • Stronger cost governance and ongoing efficiency discipline
